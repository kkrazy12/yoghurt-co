About Yogan and our work

Here at Yogan we create exquisite vegan yoghurts and desserts. We recently opened here in Plymouth, a small city in England. Rain or shine, Plymouth, Britain's Ocean City is home to breath-taking views, plenty to see and do and lots of fresh, sea air. Our customers visit regularly and we are open to their feedback and requests. We feel at home here and want you to aswell!

The vegan lifestyle is very important to us for multiple reasons. If you want to read more about the benefits of the lifestyle, please view our vegan lifestyle page. Our main priority is that our customers are satisfied with their dessert and know exactly where it has come from and how it was made. We use all natural ingreidents and cooking processes to ensure the desserts are the healthiest we can make them.

Our speciality is our wide range of yoghurts available to collect or deliver. We don't know of any other vegan yoghurt companies, and hope we can prove to you how great vegan yoghurts can taste. When you purchase a yoghurt, you will receive the dessert in fully recyclable pots and materials because we care deeply about our enivronment.

Not only do we sell yoghurts here at Yogan, but we have many different desserts and hot beverages our customers can choose from. We appreciate that it can be difficult to find vegan alternatives for the foods you love and here at Yogan, we want you to feel at home.

To cater for those who do not want to spend too much money on vegan catering services, we have created our own at a fair price. Come and visit us for a taster session ready for your big event. We understand how stressful arranging things like this can be, so we have a dedicated page you can view for more information.

Lastly, if you are interested in joining our team, please contact us at yogan@plymouth.co.uk or check out our social media linked at the bottom of the page.
